Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Littletaps ("we", "us", "the app") is built around a simple promise: your app data never leaves your phone unless you choose to send it somewhere. This policy describes what that means in practice.

What we collect

Nothing from the app on our servers. We do not run servers that store your kicks, sessions, notes, or any personal information from the app. We do not require an account, an email address, or a phone number to use the app.

What's stored on your device

The native iOS app stores the following locally on your device using SwiftData and UserDefaults:

  • Kick timestamps and session metadata (duration, status, optional notes you add)
  • Your in-app preferences (sound choice, haptics, screen-awake preference, and onboarding completion)

This data stays on your device. Uninstalling the app removes it.

Deleting data

You can delete individual saved sessions from History. You can also remove all app data by deleting Littletaps from your device. Because we do not receive your app data, we cannot retrieve or delete a server copy.

Analytics & tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, Segment, or any other analytics service. We do not include third-party SDKs that track your behavior. We do not use advertising identifiers.

CSV exports

If you choose to export your kick history as a CSV file, that file is generated locally on your device. It is then yours to share — by email, AirDrop, or any other means you choose. We never see it.

Support emails

If you email us for support or TestFlight access, we receive the email address and message you choose to send. We use that only to reply to you and manage the request. Please do not send sensitive medical information by email.

Cookies

The marketing site (this website) does not set tracking cookies. We do not use a cookie banner because we do not need one.

Website services

The marketing site loads fonts from Google Fonts. The native iOS app does not load Google Fonts, use analytics, or contact our website during normal app use.

Children's privacy

The app is intended for adults monitoring a pregnancy. It is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes materially, we'll update the date above and post a clear note on the home page.
